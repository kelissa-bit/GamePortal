Welcome to my game portal!

You get to choose between three games, a number guessing game, Spit, and a Buzzfeed Quiz.

Number Guessing Game:
In this game, you guess a number.
A  number between 1 and 100 is chosen, and you have to guess which number it could be. If you're lower, it'll tell you, and if you're higher, it'll also tell you. Using these clues, try and deduce what the number is!

Spit:
This is a card game also known as Speed.
You play against a computer and have to get rid of your deck before it does. In order to do so, you have to place a card that is one lower or one higher than what is in the deck. Unlike normal Spit, there are turns. If you can't go your turn, it switches to the computer. If the computer can't, the discard pile is rerolled until either of you can.

Buzzfeed Quiz:
This is a Buzzfeed quiz entirely in the terminal! You get questions about certain scenarios with four answer choices. Once you finish, you get a result, which is what music genre you are!
